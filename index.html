Note Digest

Repository  Issues  License 

This is a peice of software I created to help me orgainze my notes and review for tests. This software aims to understand the intent of the document writer not its meaning. It does this through a simple set of rules:

  1. Headings are in red
    1. Chapter headings are underlined twice
    2. Sub headings are underlined once
    3. The lowest level heading is not underlined
  2. Definitions are in green
    1. The defined term is underlined
    2. The defintion content goes below the term
  3. Everything else is in blue
